posture-max

posture-max helps you take control of your spine health effortlessly. Using smart sensors or your device’s camera, the app tracks your posture throughout the day and gives you clear, actionable feedback. You'll receive a real-time posture score (a percentage), a posture grade, and a breakdown of time spent in good vs. bad posture. At the end of each day, Max Posture delivers a personalized daily summary, showing your progress and areas for improvement. Over time, you’ll see trends, celebrate wins, and stay motivated with progress updates on how your posture is improving. Coming soon: posture-wars. Compete with friends to see who can maintain the best posture streaks. Because better posture is more fun with friends :).
